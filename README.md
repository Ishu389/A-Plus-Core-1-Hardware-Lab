# CompTIA A+ Core 1: Study Notes & Hands-on Hardware Lab 🚀

Welcome to my A+ Core 1 repository! I passed my Core 1 exam a couple of months back, but instead of just memorising brain dumps to pass a test, I wanted to actually build out the knowledge and document my practical labs. 

This repositry serves as a digital notebook of my theory prep and a portfolio of my hands-on hardware experiments. Employers, feel free to look around at my lab logs!

## 🛠️ What's Inside?
* **`/hardware-labs`** - Real-world teardowns, cleaning, and rebuilding of actual PC hardware.
* **`/core-1-cheat-sheets`** - High-density study guides on networking ports, RAID configurations, and troubleshooting models.

## 🧰 Core Skills Demonstrated
* **Hardware Architecture:** Full system assembly, component sourcing, and ESD safety.
* **Networking & Troubleshooting:** IP configuration, command-line diagnostics (`ping`, `ifconfig`/`ip`), and routing.
* **Storage Systems:** RAID arrays configuration and storage media deployment.
